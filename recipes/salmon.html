Baked Salmon and Vegetables recepie

This is a simple, easy to make salmon recepie that only requires an oven about 40 minutes of your time. It's low carb and keto friendly!

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillet
  • broccoli
  • lemon pepper seasoning
  • lemon

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Spray a glass baking tray with cooking spray, place salmon fillet skin side down into the tray.
  3. Season the salmon fillet with lemon pepper seasoning to taste.
  4. Bake @ 350 degrees for 40 minutes.
  5. With about 10 minutes left, place cut broccoli into a pot with a steamer basket, filled 1/3 with water, and bring to a boil.
  6. Boil for 5-6 minutes.
  7. Remove salmon from oven and discard the skin. Place onto a plate with the steamed broccoli.
  8. Coat salmon with lemon juice and serve.
